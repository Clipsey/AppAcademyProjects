    Eggs and Bacon Recipe

    Description

    How to cook Eggs and Bacon the american way. First cook the eggs, and then the bacon, and then eat.

    Directions

    1. Melt butter on pan on the stove
    2. Crack eggs and pour into pan
    3. Once eggs are done cooking, remove from pan and replace with bacon
    4. Cook bacon until crispy, remove and enjoy.
